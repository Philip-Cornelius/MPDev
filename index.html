Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I purchase a small business website with a newsletter?

    Newsletters can be added on every website at an extra cost of $50.

  • What if I want to change the information on my personal website after gaining new experience or education?

    We offer a support package that goes for $3/month that allows unlimited changes and support for your personal website. Alternatively a once off fee of $15 is charged for changes.

  • What if I want to host my website somewhere else?

    You can host the website yourself, barring the full payment is done before we hand over the project files.

  • Can you make websites for anyone across the world?

    We are international, we accept payments in various forms, barring the website is in English.

    All your specific requirements will be discussed in our free consultation with you and depending on your needs the website will be priced accordingly.
